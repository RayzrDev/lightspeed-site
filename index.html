About Us

LightspeedMC is a unique survival server focused on remaining true to Minecraft's roots, with vanilla-esque gameplay and a friendly community. Our goal is to provide an engaging environment for you to hang out, chat, make friends, and enjoy Minecraft the way it was meant to be: plain and simple.

Vanilla Gameplay

We believe that Minecraft is best enjoyed when experienced as it was originally intended, so LightspeedMC was designed to have a nearly vanilla experience, with only a few minor enhancements to keep things interesting.

Friendly Community

Nobody likes a bully or a griefer! That's why the LightspeedMC staff make sure to keep things under control so that our members can enjoy a friendly, inviting environment where they will want to stay.

24/7 Uptime

LightspeedMC is a 24/7 server, meaning you can join at any time! Feel like hopping on with your friends at 3 AM for a late night gaming session? Go for it! We look forward to seeing you.
